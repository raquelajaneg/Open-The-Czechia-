Essential advice and practical information for your perfect trip to the heart of Europe

Essential Travel Tips

Currency & Money

The official currency is the Czech Crown (CZK). Euros are accepted in some tourist areas but it's better to have local currency.

Important Notes:

  • Credit cards widely accepted in cities
  • ATMs available everywhere (avoid airport ATMs)
  • Keep small bills for public transport
  • Average daily budget: 1500-2500 CZK

Transportation
