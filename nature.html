Nature of Czech Republic

Discover the breathtaking landscapes, pristine forests, and stunning natural wonders of the heart of Europe

Wild Beauty of Bohemia and Moravia

The Czech Republic is a country of diverse natural landscapes, from rolling hills and deep forests to majestic mountains and peaceful rivers. With over 2,000 protected areas, it's a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Despite being a relatively small country, Czechia boasts incredible biodiversity, with ancient forests covering one-third of its territory and thousands of kilometers of hiking trails waiting to be explored.
