Meet the Author

Behind the stories and experiences shared on Open The Czechia

Raquel Araújo

Hello! I'm Raquel, an international student from Brazil who fell in love with the Czech Republic during my exchange program. As a Marketing student, I've always been passionate about storytelling and discovering new cultures.

"Moving to Czechia was more than just an academic exchange - it was a life-changing experience that opened my eyes to the beauty of Central Europe."

With a background in Marketing, I approach travel and cultural exploration with a unique perspective, always seeking to understand what makes each destination special. My journey from the vibrant streets of Belo Horizonte to the historic charm of Prague has been filled with incredible discoveries that I'm excited to share with you.

My Journey

My adventure in Czechia began as a one-semester exchange program, but the country's charm quickly turned it into a much longer stay. From navigating Prague's winding cobblestone streets to exploring hidden castles in the countryside, every day brought new wonders.

Why This Website Exists

During my time in Czechia, I discovered that many people only know Prague's most famous attractions. I wanted to create a platform that shows the true depth and beauty of this incredible country.

"My goal is to inspire curiosity and show that Czechia has so much more to offer than what meets the tourist's eye."

Through Open The Czechia, I combine my marketing background with my passion for travel to create engaging content that showcases the country's hidden gems, rich culture, and practical travel tips. Whether you're planning your first visit or you've been here before, I hope this website helps you discover something new and wonderful about the heart of Europe.
